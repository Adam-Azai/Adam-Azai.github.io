I'm Adam Azai, a Full Stack Developer/Software Engineer.

Hey there! I'm Adam/Imron Azai, an entry-level software engineer with a background in pre-medicine. I'm passionate about developing projects that provide practical features while also being designed with a strong emphasis on ethics and user experience.

​ Originally from San Diego, California, I grew up playing soccer and wrestling, and I have a deep love for fantasy novels. In high school, I was first introduced to software engineering through my robotics course, and I discovered a true passion for it that surpassed my interest in medicine. After college, I decided to follow my passion and pursue full stack development.

As a programmer, I thrive on challenges, and I find debugging to be particularly invigorating. I love the mental exercise of working through problems and coming up with creative solutions. I'm excited to continue building my skills and exploring new technologies in the field of software engineering. If you're interested in collaborating on a project or just want to chat about programming, feel free to reach out!
